Karachi

By Maimoona Shah Khan

The city of lights. It has a nostalgic past. It has scars all over it. It has been part of the ugly stories. It has witnessed poverty and vice. It has been victim of bloody fights. It has been through thick and thins. It has been through lows and highs. This wild baby has a soft heart with deep wounds. But, it still stands tall and high. Proud and lit every night. Growing and breathing all the time. Karachi -my muse. My home. My poem.
